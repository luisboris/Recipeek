Panada Sauce Recipe
Niru Gupta
Review 
Overview
Cook Time
Ingredients
Method
Panada sauce is a thick, milky white sauce which is usually added to bind the batter. It may be used as thickening agent in soups and bakes.
Total Cook Time 25 mins 
Prep Time 10 mins 
Cook Time 15 mins 
Recipe Servings2
Easy
Ingredients of Panada Sauce
1/3 cup flour
3 Tbsp butter or oil
1 cup milk
1 tsp salt or to taste
How to Make Panada Sauce
HideShow Media 
1.
Heat the butter/oil for the sauce and add the flour.
2.
Saute over low heat till slightly colored.
3.
Add milk, stirring vigorously all the time, so no lumps form.
4.
Bring to a boil and cook for a minute or so.
5.
Add the salt.
6.
Leave to cool.
