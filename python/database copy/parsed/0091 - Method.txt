how to make palak paratha with step by step photo:
palak puree recipe:
1. firstly, in a large vessel add enough water and get to boil. 
2. further add washed palak leaves. 
3. get the water to boil. do not over boil as palak will loose its nutritious. 
4. transfer the blanched palak into blender without adding water. 
5. also add in ginger and chilli. cool completely. 
6. further blend to smooth puree without adding any extra water. 
palak paratha dough recipe:
1. firstly, in a large bowl take 2 cups of wheat flour. 
2. further add Â½ tsp ajwain, salt to taste and 2 tsp oil. 
3. also add prepared palak puree. 
4. combine well making sure the palak gets mixed well uniformly. 
5. furthermore, add water as required and knead for 5 minutes. 
6. make sure to knead to smooth and soft dough. 
7. grease the dough with little oil. 
8. cover with moist cloth and rest for 30 minutes. 
palak paratha preparation recipe:
1. now pinch a medium sized ball dough, roll and flatten it. 
2. also dust with some wheat flour. 
3. further roll it in a thin circle like chapathi or paratha. 
4. now on a hot tawa place the rolled paratha and cook for a minute. 
5. furthermore, when the base is partly cooked, flip the palak paratha. 
6. also brush oil / ghee and press slightly. 
7. further, flip again once or twice till both the sides are cooked properly. 
8. finally, serve palak paratha with raita and pickle.
