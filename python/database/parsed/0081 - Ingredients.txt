Ingredients
4 ½-inch/1cm thick slices cheese (Edam, Gouda or Hermelín)
1 cup all-purpose flour
2 eggs , beaten
1 cup bread crumbs
2 cups vegetable oil
For the tartar sauce (tatarská omáčka)
4 tablespoons mayonnaise
1 tablespoon vinegar
1 teaspoon mustard
4 gherkins (pickles) , finely chopped
1 tablespoon capers
½ onion , grated
Salt
Freshly ground black pepper
