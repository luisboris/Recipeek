RECIPE 
Makes about 12 squares
For the crust: 
1 ⅓ cup + 2 tablespoons whole-wheat pastry flour, plus more for dusting 
¾ cup unsalted grass-fed butter, chilled (1 ½ sticks) 
1 teaspoon sea salt 
2-3 tablespoons ice-cold water 
1 egg, beaten, or 2 tablespoons heavy cream, for brushing the crust 
For the vegetable filling: 
½ medium butternut squash, peeled and thinly sliced into discs 
1 cup curly kale, roughly torn (stems removed) 
¼ red onion, thinly sliced 
2 cloves garlic, minced 
2 tablespoons unsalted grass-fed butter 
1 tablespoon olive oil 
Salt and pepper, to taste 
6 oz. havarti cheese, shredded 
⅓ cup crumbled feta cheese 
Fresh basil, for garnish 
Crushed red pepper flakes, optional
