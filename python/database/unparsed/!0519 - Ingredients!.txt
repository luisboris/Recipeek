¼ teaspoon turmeric powder
2 to 2.5 tablespoons vangi bath masala powder
salt as per taste
2 tablespoon fresh grated coconut, optional
¼ to ½ teaspoon jaggery powder
2 to 3 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves \- optional
